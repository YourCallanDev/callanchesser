Julia Caesar

Date: 23rd to 25th Oct 2023

Role: Projectionist

About the Show

For Julia Caesar, written and directed by Mr Neil Simpson, I operated all projections using PowerPoint across the technical rehearsal, dress rehearsal, and two live performances. The production featured a cast of Primary 7 students, and my role focused on ensuring the visual elements were delivered accurately and at the correct moments throughout the show.

I worked closely with the director and technical director to ensure the projections supported the storytelling and flowed smoothly with the action on stage, helping to enhance the overall visual presentation of the production.
