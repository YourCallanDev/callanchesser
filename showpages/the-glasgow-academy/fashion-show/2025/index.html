TGA Fashion 25’

Role: Technical Director

About the Event

For TGA Fashion ’25, I led the technical operations for the event, coordinating a team of nine to deliver lighting, sound, projections, cameras, and recording. The show took place in the Watson Auditorium and required careful planning and communication to ensure all technical elements worked together smoothly throughout the evening.

As a fundraising event supporting Emmaus, the production brought together students from across the school in a large-scale showcase. My role involved overseeing the setup, cue coordination, and live operation of the technical systems, ensuring the event ran seamlessly and that every segment of the show was well supported from a technical perspective.
