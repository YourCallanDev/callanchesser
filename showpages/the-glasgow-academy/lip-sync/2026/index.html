TGA Lip Sync 26'

Date: February 27th, 2026

Role: Lighting Director

About the Show

For the Teacher Lip Sync Competition 2026, I served as the Lighting (LX) Designer. Although the show primarily featured busking performances, I prepared a variety of pre-set cues using an ETC Element console to enhance key moments throughout the event.

The production was an exciting and high-energy experience, and the lighting played a key role in creating atmosphere and highlighting the performances. It was a fantastic opportunity to contribute creatively to a lively event and see how technical design can elevate the overall experience for both performers and audience members.
