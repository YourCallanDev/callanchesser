About the Event

For the 2023 BBC Children in Need Lip Sync Competition, I managed all technical aspects of the event, including equipment setup and coordination with the organisers. My responsibilities covered lighting, sound, and stage management, ensuring each performance ran smoothly and professionally.

Working on this charity event required careful planning and real-time problem solving to maintain seamless technical support, allowing performers to focus on their acts while the production ran without interruption.
