Granny Got Stuck In The Alexa

Date: 17th to 19th Dec 2024

Role: Follow Spot Operator

About the Show

For this production of Granny Got Stuck in the Alexa, I worked alongside our director, Mr Neil Simpson, supporting a main cast of P7 pupils as well as additional performers from P1–P7 who took part in the musical numbers. The production involved several technical and dress rehearsals followed by three live performances.

My role was operating the follow spot, ensuring key performers were clearly highlighted throughout the show and that lighting cues complemented the action on stage. This involved careful timing and coordination with the wider technical team to maintain focus on the performers and support the overall flow of the production.
