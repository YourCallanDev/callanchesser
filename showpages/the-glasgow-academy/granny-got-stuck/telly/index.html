Granny Got Stuck In The Telly

Date: 4th to 7th Dec 2023

Role: Stage Crew

About the Show

For Granny Got Stuck in the Telly, I supported the production as part of the backstage team during technical and dress rehearsals, as well as across two live performances. Written and directed by Mr Neil Simpson, the show featured a main cast of P7 pupils alongside additional P1–P7 performers who took part in the musical numbers.

My responsibilities included assisting with backstage coordination, helping manage performer transitions, and supporting microphone changes to ensure the show ran smoothly. Working closely with the cast and technical team, I helped maintain the pace of the production and ensured performers were ready to enter the stage at the right moments.
