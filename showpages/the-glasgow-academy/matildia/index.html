Matildia

Date: 27th to 30th of April, 2025

Role: Projection Operator

About the Show

For this production of Matildia JR, I worked as part of the technical team alongside the technical director, crew, and director Mr Neil Simpson, with the production licensed through MTI Europe. The run included a full technical rehearsal period, dress rehearsals, and six public performances. My role focused on operating the projection system using StagePlayer, ensuring all visual cues were executed accurately and in sync with the action on stage. Each performance involved coordinating projections for a large company of around 60 performers, contributing to the smooth technical running of the production.
