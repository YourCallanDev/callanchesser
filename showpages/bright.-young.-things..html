Bright. Young. Things.

Date: 12th Feb 2025

Role: Stage Manager / Tech Designer

About the Show

For my debut Drama Club production, I collaborated with our tech director Mrs. Wishart, to manage the overall technical design including sound & lighting. It was a great show to get to learn about all the formalities of the tech world, like LX plots and plans and cue maps etc. The cast was a S1-S3 lot, and I was guiding the team through connected cues and any other places where assistance was needed as well as rehearsals!
