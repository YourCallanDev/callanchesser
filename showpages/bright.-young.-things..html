Bright. Young. Things.

Date: February 12th, 2025

Role: Stage Manager/Tech Designer

About the Show

For my debut Drama Club production, we did Bright. Young. Things. It was a great show with S1-S3 performing. I did the overall design with our tech director Mrs Wishart who assisted.

Extra Notes

This was a great show to learn more about plots and different ways of using lights. We had a dress, tech, and 1 show. Great show to start with!
