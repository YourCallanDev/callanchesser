Willy Wonka : Staged

Date: 15th to 19th July 2024

Role: Technical Director

About the Show

For Willy Wonka, directed by Sophia C, I managed technical operations for a second-week summer camp production. Oversaw lighting, sound, and microphones while collaborating with the director to enhance performances for children aged 5–12, delivering smooth shows for a theatre capacity of 150.
