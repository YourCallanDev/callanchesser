Shrek : Filmed

Date: October 2024

Role: Technical Director & Editor

About the Show

For this production, instead of performing it we filmed it. Written & directed by Liam Campbell, Shrek was an amazing production. It was my first time working with Liam, and it was amazing to get the chance to collaborate.

Extra Notes

We had Shrek mic'ed up and the rest used ambient mics for a cast including 30 dancers! The video was edited in Da Vinci Resolve.
