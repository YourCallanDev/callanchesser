Granny Got Stuck In The Telly

Date: 4th to 7th Dec 2023

Role: Stage Crew

About the Show

For Granny Got Stuck In The Telly, I assisted backstage during technical and dress rehearsals as well as two performances. The production, written and directed by Mr Neil Simpson, involved a main cast of P7s with contributions from P1–P7 in songs, and I helped ensure smooth transitions and mic changes for the performers.
