Harry Potter : Staged

Date: 8th to 12th July 2024

Role: Technical Director

About the Show

For this summer camp production of Harry Potter (and my SiTC debut), directed by Sophia C, I led the technical department for a cast of children aged 5–12. My responsibilities included managing microphones, sound, and lighting, while adapting to space limitations to ensure smooth rehearsals and performances for a theatre capacity of 150.
