Harry Potter : Staged

Date: 8th to 12th of July, 2024

Role: Technical Director

About the Show

For my company debut, it was wonderful to join and lead the tech dept. of Harry Potter. Written & directed by Sophia C, it was my first camp so I was trying to involve as much as possible.

Extra Notes

Kids aged 5-12 performing with a theatre capacity of 150. Used personal and ambient mics to handle space access issues.
