Snow White & The Magnificent Eight

Date: 22nd to 23rd Nov 2025

Role: Technical Director / Sound Mixer

About the Show

For Snow White & The Magnificent Eight, my debut production with Drumchapel Amateur Pantomime Club, I was responsible for both the technical direction and live sound mixing. My role included designing and programming lighting and sound cues, coordinating the technical logistics of the production, and operating the show throughout rehearsals and performances alongside a small crew. The microphone setup was complex, requiring a detailed mic plot and precise live mixing, including extensive fader management to ensure smooth transitions between performers.
