Snow White & The Magnificent Eight

Date: 22nd to 23rd of November, 2025

Role: Technical Director/Sound Mixer

About the Show

For my debut amateur production at Drumchapel Panto Club, I was the Technical Director and Sound Mixer. As the technical director it was my job to design, and arrange all logistical things. This show had a lot of cool lighting effects and cues, as well as the use of mics for every character.

Extra Notes

I had two additional crew to help me (on-book SM, and LX operator). During rehearsals every Sunday I would attend and sort logistics and practice operation. This was a very fun debut performance!
