Snow White & The Magnificent Eight

Date: 22nd to 23rd Nov 2025

Role: Technical Director / Sound Mixer

About the Show

For Snow White & The Magnificent Eight, my debut production at Drumchapel Amateur Pantomime Club, I handled technical direction and sound mixing. I was desiging lighting & sound cues, arranging technical logistics, and operated the show with support from a small crew during rehearsals and performances. The mic plot was advanced and involved fader throwing.
