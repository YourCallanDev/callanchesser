About the Show

For Granny Got Stuck In The Time Machine, directed and written by Mr Neil Simpson, I designed and operated all projections using QLab. I also served as Stage Manager On Book for four performances, and helping the tech team out wherever needed. With the main P7 cast as well as P1–P7 participants in ensemble numbers.
