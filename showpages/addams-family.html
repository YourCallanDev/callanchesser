Addams Family : Young Part

Date: May to Aug 2025

Role: Projectionist

About the Show

For this production of, The Addams Family : Young Part I was working alongside the technical director & crew, as well as our director Mr Neil Simpson with the production written by MTI Europe. The run included technical and dress rehearsals as well as six performances. I was operating the projections using StagePlayer, with a company of 60 on the stage per night.
