Shrek : Filmed

Date: October 2024

Role: Tech Director / Editor

About the Show

For the filmed production of Shrek, directed & written by Liam Campbell, I managed technical elements and edited the full production in Da Vinci Resolve. I oversaw microphone setup for the main cast and ambient mics for additional dancers, coordinating a combined cast of around 38 performers.
