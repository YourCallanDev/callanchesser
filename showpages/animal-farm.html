Animal Farm

Date: 11th of February, 2026

Role: Technical Designer/AV Operator

About the Show

For Animal Farm, my second/final TGA drama club show I designed the all the technical aspects (bar act 2 lighting), which was lights, sound, and projections as well as helping with props and set. It was an amazing opertunity, and we had an amazing cast. Working with them from September, its been great to also follow them through rehearsals and from then planning our lighting ideas and video etc ideas to being able to put on a 10/10 show! It was also great to get to learn a bit more about how things work, from our tech director/show director Mrs Wishart!
