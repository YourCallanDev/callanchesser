Julia Caesar

Date: 23rd to 25th Oct 2023

Role: Projectionist

About the Show

For Julia Caesar, written and directed by Mr Neil Simpson, I operated all projections using QLab across a dress rehearsal, technical rehearsal, and two performances. The cast consisted of Primary 7 students, and I worked closely with the director and technical director to ensure the visual storytelling enhanced the production.
