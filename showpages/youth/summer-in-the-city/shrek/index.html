Shrek : Filmed

Date: October 2024

Role: Tech Director / Editor

About the Show

For the filmed production of Shrek, directed and written by Liam Campbell, I managed all technical elements and handled the full post-production editing in DaVinci Resolve. My responsibilities included setting up and managing microphones for the main cast, as well as ambient microphones for additional dancers, coordinating a combined cast of approximately 38 performers.

This role required careful planning and attention to detail to ensure high-quality audio and video capture, as well as seamless integration of all technical elements, resulting in a polished and professional final production.
