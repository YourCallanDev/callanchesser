Wicked : Staged

Date: 4th to 8th Aug 2025

Role: Technical Director

About the Show

For Wicked: Part 1, directed and written by Liam Campbell, I managed lights and sound for a summer debut production. I installed a new analogue speaker system and coordinated lighting across the auditorium, ensuring children aged 5–12 were fully supported throughout rehearsals and live performances for an audience of approximately 150.

My role involved close attention to technical details and hands-on operation, creating a safe and engaging environment for young performers while delivering a polished and immersive experience for the audience.
