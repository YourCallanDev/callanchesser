Harry Potter : Staged

Date: 8th to 12th July 2024

Role: Technical Director

About the Show

For this summer camp production of Harry Potter—marking my SiTC debut—directed by Sophia C, I led the technical department for a cast of children aged 5–12. My responsibilities included managing microphones, sound, and lighting, while adapting to the constraints of the performance space to ensure smooth rehearsals and live shows for an audience of approximately 150.

This role required careful planning and flexibility, supporting young performers and helping bring the magical world of the production to life through precise technical coordination and live show management.
