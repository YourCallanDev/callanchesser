KPOP Demon Hunters : Filmed

Date: 14th to 17th Oct 2025

Role: Editorial Director

About the Show

For the filmed production of KPOP Demon Hunters, directed and written by Liam Campbell, I managed the full editing process using DaVinci Resolve, ensuring the final video was polished and cohesive.

For children who were not involved in filming on certain days, I facilitated learning about production elements and guided them in creating, rehearsing, and performing a small play. This allowed all participants to engage creatively and gain hands-on experience in theatre and film production.
