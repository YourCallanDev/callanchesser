Moana : Staged

Date: 11th to 15th Aug 2025

Role: Technical Director

About the Show

For this production of Moana, directed by Liam Campbell, I managed all aspects of live audio, including advanced sound mixing and individually mic’ing up each actor according to detailed mic plots. I also oversaw lighting and other technical elements, working closely with the director and additional operators to enhance the overall technical design while adapting to the limitations of the performance space.

My role involved coordinating numerous additional elements—cue management, live mixing, and supporting performers with microphones—ensuring smooth rehearsals and a polished live performance for an audience of approximately 150. This hands-on approach allowed the technical aspects to elevate the storytelling and create a fully immersive experience for both performers and audience members.
