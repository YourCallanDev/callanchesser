Into The Woods JR

Date: 17th to 18th of June, 2026

Role: Sound Operator

About the Show

For Folksy Youth Theatre's *Into The Woods JR* at Rutherglen Town Hall, I was the Sound Operator for the production. My responsibilities included creating and programming the QLab file containing all music and sound effect cues, operating sound throughout the performances, and assisting with troubleshooting technical issues as they arose. The production included a full get-in and get-out, technical and dress rehearsals, and two live performances. During the production week, I also assisted with removing steel deck staging, setting up and operating a Chinese smoke machine, programming radio frequencies on walkie talkies for the production team, and acting as a runner to support the smooth operation of the show. This was a valuable experience that allowed me to further develop my skills in live sound operation, playback programming, and backstage technical support while working as part of a production team to deliver a successful musical theatre performance.
