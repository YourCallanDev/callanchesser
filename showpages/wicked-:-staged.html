Wicked : Staged

Date: 4th to 8th Aug 2025

Role: Technical Director

About the Show

For Wicked, directed & written by Liam Campbell, I managed lights and sound for a summer debut production. I installed a new analogue speaker systems and coordinated lighting across the auditorium, ensuring children aged 5–12 were effectively supported across rehearsals and performances for an audience of 150.
