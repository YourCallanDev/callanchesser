Hey, thanks for stopping by!

Theatre technician specialising in lighting, programming, technical direction and live production support across theatre, concerts and events.

Hi, I’m Callan Chesser, a 15-year-old technical theatre practitioner who’s been lucky enough to work on over 25 productions so far — from pantomimes and drama club shows to school talent shows and summer camps.

Over the last few years I’ve directed and managed TGA charity productions, led crews of up to 35 people, programmed and called live shows, designed lighting states, mixed microphones for large casts, and supported productions in both school and professional venues.

Some of my favourite experiences include designing lighting for fashion and talent shows, running live sound and mic plots, building and maintaining LED video walls, and transferring productions from StagePlayer to QLab when systems fail.
