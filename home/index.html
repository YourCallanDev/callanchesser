Hey, I'm Callan Chesser.

Hi, I'm a 15-year-old student technician who's been lucky enough to work on over 25 productions so far — from pantomimes and drama shows to school talent shows and summer camps. I love everything about theatre: lighting, sound, projections, stage management, and everything backstage.

I've spent the last few years directing and managing TGA's charity shows, running crews of up to 35 people, designing and calling shows, and making sure every performance goes smoothly. Outside theatre, I'm a competitive national swimmer, so teamwork, focus, and calm under pressure are second nature.
