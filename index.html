I am Callan Chesser, a student technician. I am currently in secondary school and work on projects in and out of school. I love helping out in lighting, sound, backstage, and everything theatre because it brings so much enjoyment to me. I started helping out in 2023, and since then I have continued to work on projects! One of the places I help is Summer In The City — a summer camp that also offers a theatrical camp. If you’re interested, get in touch or check out some of my credits!
